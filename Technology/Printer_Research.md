# ✅ Printer Research Checklist

↖️ [Return to the main file](../README.md)

### Type and Features

- [ ] Decide between inkjet, laser, or thermal printer
- [ ] Check printing speed (pages per minute)
- [ ] Review color vs. black and white options
- [ ] Look for required features (scanning, copying, faxing)

### Connectivity

- [ ] Verify Wi-Fi and Bluetooth capabilities
- [ ] Check USB connection options
- [ ] Review mobile printing support
- [ ] Confirm driver availability for your OS

### Cost Considerations

- [ ] Compare initial purchase price
- [ ] Research ink or toner costs
- [ ] Check yield per cartridge
- [ ] Evaluate total cost of ownership

### Size and Space

- [ ] Measure available desk or shelf space
- [ ] Consider paper tray capacity
- [ ] Review noise level specifications
- [ ] Check warranty and support options
