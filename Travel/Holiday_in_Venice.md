# ✅ Holiday in Venice Checklist

↖️ [Return to the main file](../README.md)

### Before Departure

- [ ] Check passport validity
- [ ] Book accommodations and transportation
- [ ] Research local attractions and restaurants
- [ ] Purchase travel insurance

### Packing

- [ ] Pack appropriate clothing for the weather
- [ ] Bring comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Include camera and chargers
- [ ] Prepare travel documents

### During Trip

- [ ] Explore famous landmarks (St. Mark's Basilica, Grand Canal)
- [ ] Take a gondola ride
- [ ] Try local cuisine
- [ ] Visit local museums and galleries

### Return Home

- [ ] Collect all belongings
- [ ] Share photos and memories
- [ ] Rest and recover from travel
